About WattSaver

WattSaver aims to give users an idea about their current electrical usage. Through this, it hopes to encourage consumers to be more proactive in saving electricity, as electricity is one of the major contributors to pollution on our planet Earth. We provide calculators that are able to estimate your current electrical usage by only taking the most minimal amount of information to give you a general idea on what to do with your current electrical usage. We highly encourage you to use one of these calculators right now!

Our Inspiration

Throughout our modern times, especially during this era, it is no secret that electrical appliances have become a necessary part of our lives. One of our group members, decided one day to flip open his physics textbook, when he saw a table comparing the differences between CFL and LED light bulbs. Armed with this new information, he decided to construct an online calculator, so that the public can use it and determine the impact of their daily lives.
